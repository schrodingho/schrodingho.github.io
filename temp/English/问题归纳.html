问题归纳

小作文

  • 开头改写->改写为从句

  • the 的用法

    • 用 the
      • 独一无二：the earth / the sun / the moon
      • the piano / the violin / play the drum
      • 所说事物之前已经被提到: I’m from Shanghai.The city is very busy.
      • 所说之物在说话者之间有某种共识: We went to the park yesterday
      • the elderly / the young / the rich / the poor
    • 不能用 the
      • 泛指某物、某种大概念: I like dogs (复数)
      • home 和 school 前一般不加 the
      • 第一次出现 用 a/an

  • 一直比xx高

    • A is consistently higher than sth
  • reach近义词
    • stand at / reach at
    • peak at / reach its peak at / reach its lowest point at
  • 保持
    • remain stable at / remain steady at
  • see / witness / experience
    • see a significant/sharp/considerable/noticeable increase
    • witness an opposite trend
  • quantity, amount
    • the amount of +不可数名词单数形式
    • The figure for +(可数名词的复数) (不可数名词的单数)
  • 占份额
    • account for
      • sth accounted for 36% of total energy consumption in 2017.
    • make up
    • represent
      • The cost of equipment represented 23% of the school budget.
    • constitute
  • 倍数
    • sth be twice as high(long, popular) as sth
      • In European countries, the average meat consumption was twice as high as the global average
    • be three times as high as
    • doubled / tripled
      • The sales of sth in Switzerland doubled from 3 million to 6 million euros.
  • dramatic 剧烈的，急剧的
    • fluctuate - fluctuations
    • a dramatic increase / increase dramatically
    • rapid / rapidly ; sharp / sharply
    • significant / sharp / considerable / noticeable
  • steady / consistent / gradual

    table题

  • 两个时间点：2000、2010：同段横向对比
  • 多个属性：每个属性分开对比，一个属性一段

    线图题

  • 一般是时间横轴，多个属性，按照时间主线写，属性在时间内描绘
  • By 1972 ：截至1972年

    柱状图

  • 对比：while, whereas
  • 时序：多个年份的柱状图，分属性分段写
  • （时序上：线图一般并行，柱状图一般分组）

    饼图

  • 时序上同理：分组写时序

大作文

  • ad / disad ; pros / cons ; positive / negative
    • 开头
      • 讲清楚 优点 多，还是 缺点 多
        • more positive effects than negative effects.
        • I think sth create more benefits than drawbacks (problems).
        • I think this is a positive trend
        • the advantages of sth outweight the disadvantages.
    • 中间段转折
      • On the other hand, I believe that the advantages of this trend outweigh its disadvantages.
      • Despite aforementioned disadvantages, I am convinced that there is much more advantages.
      • Although…, I still deeply convinced that…
      • On the other hand, the advantages of this trend is more significant.
    • 论点展开
      • 陈述现象 -> 原因 -> 举例 -> 对比 -> 结果
      • The main advantage is that …
      • Recommend: This trend has two main benefits. The first benefit is … ; The second benefit is …
