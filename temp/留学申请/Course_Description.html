Course Description

Mathematics and Modeling

  1. Calculus I+II
    • Credit: 11(6+5)
    • Hour: 128
    • Content:
      • As an important compulsory basic course of various kinds of engineering students in our university, Calculus I is to develop students’ ability of rational thought and innovation. It provides the basic concepts of one-variable calculus and ordinary differential equations, and necessary theoretical foundation and common computational methods. This course consists of functions, limits, continuity, derivatives and integrations of one variable functions, and ordinary differential equations. Calculus II is to develop students’ ability of rational thought and innovation. It provides the basic concepts of multi-variable calculus and power series and necessary theoretical foundation and common computational methods. This course consists of differential of multi-variable functions, multiple integrals, curve integrals, surface integrals and power series.
  2. Linear algebra and space analytic geometry
    • Credit: 4
    • Hour: 64
    • Content:
      • “Linear algebra and space analytic geometry” is a public basic course of engineering, medicine, agriculture, economy and management sciences in University. The main contents of this course include: matrix and linear equations, determinant, geometrical space, vector space, eigenvalue and eigenvector, quadratic form and quadric surface. This course is the basis for calculus, mathematical experiment, mathematical physical equation, probability and statistics, matrix theory, functional analysis, mathematical model etc., but also the basis for the physical and engineering, economics and management professional courses.
  3. Probability and mathematical statistics (修改)
    • Credit: 3.5
    • Hour: 80
    • Content:
      • Probability and mathematical statistics is a branch of mathematics to study the statistical regularity of random phenomena, higher school science and engineering professional teaching plan is a main basic course. Through the studying of this course, the students master the basic concepts and mathematical methods of probability theory and mathematical statistics theory, the students formed from deterministic thinking into random thinking, establish the study of random phenomena of science thought. The cultivation of students’ ability to solve practical problems, for the follow-up courses and engaged in engineering work and scientific research work to lay a necessary theory and stochastic analysis ability.
  4. Mathematical Modeling
    • Credit: 2
    • Hour: 32
    • Content:
  5. Mathematics Experiment (修改)
    • Credit: 2
    • Hour: 32
    • Content:
      • The main content includes MATLAB language programming foundation, symbolic calculation and calculus experiment, linear algebra experiment, numerical calculation experiment, optimization model experiment, random simulation experiment. Its characteristics is based on MATLAB language programming, through the application of cases to enhance students’ understanding of mathematical methods, mathematical models, mathematical experimental thinking, and mathematical modeling thinking.
  6. Signals and Systems (修改) (Laplace and Fourier Transform)
    • Credit: 4.5
    • Hour: 72
    • Content:
      • This course is a very important basic course for electronic information engineering discipline. The concepts and techniques that form the core of the subject are of fundamental importance in all engineering disciplines. The audience of the course “signals and systems” is no longer limited to the practitioners of the traditional radio-technology discipline. Now almost all of the electronic and electrical engineering and part of the machinery, dynamics disciplines have opened the course to different extent. The course of “Signals and systems” not only is an essential element in an engineering program but also can be one of the most rewarding, exciting, and useful courses that engineering students take during their undergraduate education. With emphasis on combining theory teaching with engineering practice, fully stimulate students’ interest in learning and pay attention to the cultivation of students’ learning ability, innovation consciousness and engineering accomplishment.

    • Credit: 3
    • Hour: 48
    • Content:
      • This course is a major course of Computer Science, and is a compulsory basic course. The preceding courses include Digital Logic, Design and Applications of Large Scale Integrated Circuit, Data Structure, etc., while the subsequent courses include Principles and Interface of Microcomputer, Architecture of Computer System, Embedded System and Applications. This course serves as a connecting link between the preceding and following courses of hardware, and also a prerequisite for study of software courses, such as Operating Systems and Assembly Language Programming, etc.

2019 Spring

  • Foundations of Electronic Circuits (add electronics)
    • This course is a subject-centered course in communication engineering. The course introduces the methods of electronic circuit analysis and design. The course content includes the circuit and the circuit model, circuit analysis method, the basic properties of the amplifying circuit, frequency characteristic analysis of amplifying circuit, the negative feedback technology, signal operation and processing circuit, signal generation and conversion circuit, AC/DC power supply, etc.
  • Experiment of Electronic Circuits (?)
    • This course is an base experiment course in communication engineering. Based on “Basis of analog circuit”, this course not only serves for the theory courses, but also aims at helping students have a perceptual cognition on electronic engineering projects. Students in this course will be able to know about basic electronic components, use electronic measurement devices, handle simple electronic measurement methods, and design and build the basic circuit unit. This course highlights the design, build, test and measurement of function circuit.
  • Physics I

2019 Fall

  • Physics II
  • Digital Logic Circuit and System (?)
    • Textbook: Digital Design and Computer Architecture / Elsevier / David Money Harris, Sarah L. Harris
    • This course is the fundamental for digital design and computer science. The main content includes logic, binaries,combinational logic and sequential logic circuit design. Meanwhile, computer architecture knowledge including CPU, memory, I/O and complie language are taught. Hardware description language Verilog HDL are taught and used in the final design project to design a MIPS cpu.
  • Principles of The Computer System (?)
    • Introduction to computer design and operating system. Use STM32 as an example to construct embedded system.
    • Operating system structures, process and thread, CPU scheduling, process synchronization, deadlocks, memory management, file systems, I/O systems and device driver, mass-storage structure and disk scheduling, case studies. Introduction to computer design process; performance and cost analysis; instruction set design; data-path and controller design; pipelining; memory system; I/O design; GPU architecture and programming; introduction to advanced topics.
  • Experiment of Digital Application Circuit (?)
    • Digital Circuit Experiment is the experiment course that matches with “Digital Logic Design and Application” .On the basis of mastering the basic theoretical knowledge, analysis and design method of digital logic circuit, the course mainly focuses on how to design serial digital logic circuit, sequential digital logic circuit and digital logic system (processor) series by Verilog HDL, how to simulate and synthesize them by QUARTUS and how to implement on FPGA. The content contains two units, the first unit mainly introduce design, simulation and debugging of combinational logic circuit and timing logic circuit. The second unit named processor design which covers the complex digital logic system design, simulation and FPGA implementation of the whole process.

  • Database
    • This course is a disciplinary extensive course in network engineering and Internet of things engineering, and an elective course in communication engineering. Database system is a software system for data storage, management, processing and maintenance. It is a core component of the modern computing environment. This course introduces the basic concepts, principles, methods and applications of the database and software engineering, explains the relational database and SQL, and discusses the design and development process of database application and application software in detail. Through this course, students will understand the basic knowledge of database system; learn fundamental concepts and principles of software engineering; grasp the process, theory and development technology of database design; initially with the ability to develop the database application and high quality software efficiently, and the ability of the database system analyze and design.
